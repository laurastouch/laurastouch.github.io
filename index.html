Once upon a time...

there was a girl who wanted to grow up and be independent, so she went to live alone. To really feel at home, she began to decorate her little house with handmade tiny little objects; to let everyone know that that was her house, she hanged a beautiful garland on the front door.
With the passing of seasons, the girl was changing all the decorations, always creating new ones, with special attention to the door garland. One day, a little child, by the hand of her mom, stopped in front of the garland and, smiling, said: «I can’t wait for Christmas!»
From that day on I decided to share my passion with those who want a handmade decoration, made with the heart.
