About me

I want to tell you something about me. When I was young I travelled a lot with my parents, especially in the South of Italy; here, thank to my mother, I discovered ancient Roman art. In the “Villa” of Piazza Armerina (Sicily) I felt in love with art, ancient buildings and restoration. I was only eight years old, but I knew what my way would be: I studied hard and I enrolled at the University of Venice; in 2011 I graduated in Chemical Sciences for Conservation and Restoration. During my studies I cultivated a passion for art, colours, beauty and harmony. From the childhood until now, I have always draw, created handmade thing such as gift for my parents and made every sort of DIY! Then the drama: I couldn’t find a job as a restorer. To earn some money I worked as a secretary for few years, but it was not my path, I wasn’t happy. After three long (long, long, long… ) years working as a secretary, I was on the verge of exhaustion (/ on the brink of depression?????), my DIY where my only escape from the sad person I became. Finally I decided: I gave it all! I left my family, my friends and I changed town with a new aim: follow my heart! I literally followed my boyfriend who moved abroad and I began this new work: I create small objects, jewels and party favors. Today I’m very happy! I have a new incredible life, in a new town (Como), with new friends and a world of opportunities!
